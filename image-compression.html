How Our Image Compressor Works

Our tool uses advanced algorithms to reduce image file size while maintaining visual quality. Here's how to use it:

  1. Upload your image - Drag and drop or click to browse files
  2. Adjust settings - Choose compression level and output format
  3. Compress - Click the compress button to optimize your image
  4. Download - Save the compressed image to your device

Why Compress Images?

  • Faster websites - Smaller images load quicker, improving user experience
  • Save bandwidth - Reduce data usage for mobile users
  • Better SEO - Page speed is a ranking factor for search engines
  • Storage efficiency - Store more images with less space

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service really free?

Yes, our image compression tool is completely free to use with no hidden charges. We support our service through advertisements.

What image formats are supported?

We support JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats. The WebP format often provides the best compression with good quality.

Is there a file size limit?

You can compress images up to 10MB in size. For larger files, consider resizing first.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, all processing happens in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy.

What's the best compression level to use?

We recommend starting with 80% quality. You can adjust based on your needs - higher for better quality, lower for smaller files.
