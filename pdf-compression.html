How Our PDF Compressor Works

Our tool uses advanced algorithms to reduce PDF file size while maintaining document quality. Here's how to use it:

  1. Upload your PDF - Drag and drop or click to browse files
  2. Adjust settings - Choose compression level and options
  3. Compress - Click the compress button to optimize your PDF
  4. Download - Save the compressed PDF to your device

Why Compress PDFs?

  • Email attachments - Many email services have size limits for attachments
  • Faster uploads - Smaller files upload quicker to websites and cloud services
  • Save storage space - Reduce file sizes on your computer or server
  • Web optimization - Faster loading PDFs on your website

Frequently Asked Questions

Is PDF compression safe?

Yes, our tool performs lossless compression by default, meaning your document content remains unchanged. For stronger compression, some image quality may be reduced.

Is there a file size limit?

You can compress PDFs up to 50MB in size. For larger files, consider splitting the PDF first.

Are my PDFs stored on your servers?

No, all processing happens in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy.

Does compression affect text quality?

Text remains sharp and clear in most cases. Only embedded images may lose some quality with strong compression settings.

What's the best compression level to use?

We recommend starting with the "Balanced" mode. For maximum compression, use "Strong" mode but check the results carefully.
