How Our Image Converter Works

Our tool makes it easy to convert images between different formats while optionally compressing them. Here's how to use it:

  1. Upload your image - Drag and drop or click to browse files
  2. Select output format - Choose from JPEG, PNG, WebP, GIF, BMP, TIFF, or SVG
  3. Adjust quality - Set compression level (lower = smaller file)
  4. Convert - Click the convert button to process your image
  5. Download - Save the converted image to your device

Supported Formats

  • JPEG - Best for photographs, supports compression
  • PNG - Lossless format with transparency support
  • WebP - Modern format with excellent compression
  • GIF - Supports animation, limited to 256 colors
  • BMP - Uncompressed, large file sizes
  • TIFF - High quality, used in professional photography
  • SVG - Vector format, scales infinitely without quality loss

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service really free?

Yes, our image converter is completely free to use with no hidden charges. We support our service through advertisements.

What image formats are supported?

We support all major formats: JPEG, PNG, WebP, GIF, BMP, TIFF, and SVG. You can convert between any of these formats.

Is there a file size limit?

You can convert images up to 10MB in size. For larger files, consider resizing first.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, all processing happens in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy.

What's the best format to use?

It depends on your needs: WebP for best compression, PNG for transparency, JPEG for photos, and SVG for vector graphics.
